Mansfield Elementary School

About: Mansfield Elementary is a Kindergarten through 6th grade school located in Mansfield, New Jersey. It was a tiny school in a rural town, but the teachers were great and they helped shaped me into who I am today.

Favorite Class: Greek Mythology

My favorite memory from Mansfield was when I hit a home run during my first Little League baseball game. I got lucky it went out of the park, because I just watched the ball soar instead of actually running, so I would be doomed if it landed in play.

Warren Hills Middle School

About: Warren Hills Middle School is a 7th through 8th grade school located in Washington, New Jersey. It had four sending disctricts, Mansfield being one of them. Honestly, middle school overall was my least favorite period. The classes and teachers felt relatively boring and unimportant. Many activities and sports were canceled due to lack of funding.

Favorite Class: Web Design

My favorite memory from middle school was when I "broke the ice" at the first dance. As the stereotype, all of the boys and girls were awkwardly standing on opposite sites of the room. My friend pushed me out into the middle and got the guys to cheer me on, so I danced to whatever middle school hit was playng with the worst, whitest moves possible. Everyone had a laugh and eventually joined in, probably because their embarassment was gone, knowing they couldn't embarass themselves worse than I did after that.

Warren Hills High School

About: Warren Hills High School is a 9th through 12th grade school located in Washington, New Jersey. It had five sending districts, Mansfield being one of them. I really enjoyed high school, especially compared to middle school. There were plenty of classes to choose from, and many many clubs and sports teams to join. I was very involved back in high school, with things such as the soccer team, robotics team, jazz club, marching band, key club, habitat for humanity, and more.

Favorite Class: AP Computer Science

My favorite memory from high school was winning the SteelStacks music festival in Bethlehem, PA with my school's Jazz Band. I played the alto saxophone and was extremely nervous going in, as the venue was very professional and the other groups attending were very good. Yet, the band played our best, and ended up winning with our chosen three songs. The saxophone section also won "Best Saxophone Section," which was awesome. We then got to return at a later date to perform alongside a professional jazz band and be recorded on television!

Stevens Institute of Technology

Stevens Institute of Technology is a private research institution located in Hoboken, New Jersey. I have definitely enjoyed my time here, and am happy to live in a nice town with plenty of things to do. Being so close to New York City has shown me so many opportunities, and I hope to live and work there when I graduate.

Favorite Class: Web Programming

My favorite memory so far at Stevens had to be Syllabus week of Sophomore year. I was staying in a Stevens Leased apartment, where I met so many other cool people going here that lived nearby. During Syllabus week, naturally we had so little work, so we were able to just hang out with no worries in the world and party at night, and even headed into NYC a few times that week for the nightlife. I've never been so free of stress and responsibilities as I felt that week.
